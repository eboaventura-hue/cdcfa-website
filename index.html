Our Programs

Programs for Every Stage

We provide subsidized services to make quality early childhood education accessible to children and families within the communities where they live. Through the grants we obtain, many of our families can benefit from Free or Low-Cost services.

Ages 18 mo – 5 yrs

Center Based Child Development

We have 3 centers (Lomita, Torrance, Wilmington) providing Early Childhood Development programs for children ages 18 months to 5 years old.

Our teachers appropriately support children at their current developmental level and gently extend their thinking and reasoning to the next level, paving the way for school and future success.

Kinder – 12 yrs

School-Age

Our Torrance center offers a school-age program for children kinder through 12 years old. We are aligned with the Torrance Unified School District's academic calendar to offer after-school services and full-day services during school breaks (Spring, Summer, and Winter).

Ages 3 mo – 5 yrs

Family Child Care Homes (FCCH)

We have a network of 50+ Family Child Care Home Providers throughout Los Angeles County serving children as young as 3 months old and up to 5 years old.

Home Providers are licensed (8–14 children) day care centers and qualified early child development educators.

Our team of Hub Leaders provide field-based support and visit Home Providers twice a month. Hub Leaders provide support with developmentally engaging activities, completing DRDP assessments, and collaborating with Providers to meet the needs of children and families.

All Ages

Nutrition Services

Our Central Kitchen located in Carson cooks fresh meals daily and delivers 3 meals to our centers: Breakfast, Lunch, and PM Snack.

We operate in accordance with the Child and Adult Care Food Program (CACFP) to provide nutritious and diverse meals.

Menus are provided to families in 3 languages: English, Spanish, and Portuguese. Our families and staff contribute recipes which are included on our menus every Thursday.

How Enrollment Works 🗺️

Here's what to expect after you apply!

1
Submit Your Application

Fill out the online form or download the PDF and email/drop it off at any location.

2
Waitlist Placement

Your rank is set by eligibility priorities from the CA Dept. of Education and/or CA Dept. of Social Services.

3
We Contact You

When a space opens matching your family's needs, we'll reach out right away.

4
Submit Documents

Provide proof of need, income, and eligibility. Enrollment is confirmed after verification.
