What We Offer

Programs for Every Stage

From first steps to after school — we meet your family where you are! 🌈

🍼 Ages 3 months – 5 years

Family Child Care Home

Licensed home-based providers offer warm, personalized infant and toddler care in a cozy family-like setting — tailored to your child's unique needs.

🎨 Ages 2 – 5 years

Toddler & Preschool

Our center-based program uses the High/Scope Curriculum — supporting cognitive, language, social, emotional, and physical growth through joyful play-based discovery.

📚 Ages 5 – 12 years

After-School Care

Safe, enriching after-school care combining homework support, creative projects, and social development in a welcoming, community-centered space.

Our Story

A Community Built for Kids

The Community Development Center, Inc. believes that children learn by doing. We create warm, stimulating environments where every child is supported to explore, question, and grow with confidence.

  • 🌿 Safe, healthy environments for children and families
  • 🤝 Parents as the child's primary teacher — always included
  • 🏘️ Rooted in community — serving real needs
  • 📖 High/Scope Curriculum — experiential, hands-on learning

How Enrollment Works 🗺️

Here's what to expect after you apply!

1
Submit Your Application

Fill out the online form or download the PDF and email/drop it off at any location.

2
Waitlist Placement

Your rank is set by eligibility priorities from the CA Dept. of Education and/or CA Dept. of Social Services.

3
We Contact You

When a space opens matching your family's needs, we'll reach out right away.

4
Submit Documents

Provide proof of need, income, and eligibility. Enrollment is confirmed after verification.

5
Welcome to the Family! 🎉

Your child joins our warm, nurturing community. We can't wait to meet them!
